The Science

Backed by peer-reviewed research

Our thermal therapies aren't trends—they're evidence-based practices with decades of clinical research.

Sauna Therapy

40% Lower all-cause mortality with 4-7x weekly sauna use
50% Reduced risk of fatal cardiovascular disease
65% Lower risk of sudden cardiac death

Key Findings

  • A 20-year Finnish study of 2,315 men found that regular sauna bathing (4-7x/week) was associated with substantially lower cardiovascular and all-cause mortality.
  • Sauna use triggers heat shock proteins (HSPs) that protect cells, repair damaged proteins, and support longevity pathways.
  • Regular sauna increases plasma volume by up to 7%, improving endurance performance and cardiovascular efficiency.
  • Regular sauna sessions show improved cardiac function and reduced blood pressure within 2-4 weeks of consistent use.

Laukkanen et al. "Association Between Sauna Bathing and Fatal Cardiovascular and All-Cause Mortality Events" — JAMA Internal Medicine, 2015

Cold Water Immersion

250% Increase in dopamine levels lasting 2+ hours
300% Spike in norepinephrine (focus & energy)
3°C Optimal temperature for maximum benefit

Key Findings

  • Cold water immersion at 14°C for 1 hour increases dopamine by 250%—the same magnitude as cocaine but sustained for hours without crash.
  • Norepinephrine increases 200-300% within minutes, enhancing focus, mood, and energy naturally.
  • Cold exposure activates brown adipose tissue, increasing metabolic rate and supporting healthy body composition.
  • Regular cold exposure reduces inflammation markers (IL-6, TNF-α) and supports immune function.

Šrámek et al. "Human physiological responses to immersion into water of different temperatures" — European Journal of Applied Physiology, 2000

Therapeutic Hot Baths

28% Lower risk of cardiovascular disease
26% Reduced risk of stroke with daily bathing
140% Increase in growth hormone release

Key Findings

  • A Japanese study of 30,000+ participants found daily hot bath immersion reduced cardiovascular disease risk by 28% compared to 1-2x weekly.
  • Hot water immersion mimics cardiovascular exercise: increased heart rate, blood flow, and cardiac output.
  • Passive heating through hot baths increases nitric oxide production, improving arterial flexibility and blood pressure.
  • Heat exposure stimulates growth hormone release and cortisol modulation, supporting recovery and stress resilience.

Ukai et al. "Habitual tub bathing and risks of incident coronary heart disease and stroke" — Heart (BMJ), 2020

Hyperbaric Oxygen (HBOT)

50% Faster wound healing in clinical trials
38% Improved cognitive function in aging study
2.0 ATA Pressure delivering 10-15x normal oxygen

Key Findings

  • HBOT delivers oxygen at pressures that dissolve 10-15x more O₂ into blood plasma, reaching tissues that red blood cells cannot.
  • Studies show HBOT reduces pro-inflammatory cytokines and supports endothelial (blood vessel) function.
  • A Tel Aviv University study found HBOT increased telomere length by 20% and reduced senescent cells by 37% in aging adults.
  • FDA-approved for 14 conditions; researched for TBI, cognitive decline, and sports recovery.

Hadanny et al. "Hyperbaric oxygen therapy increases telomere length and decreases immunosenescence" — Aging (Albany NY), 2020

Common Questions

Everything you need to know

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out to our team.

What's included in a Day Pass?

Full access to our thermal circuit (sauna, banya, ice baths, therapeutic hot baths), cold plunge pools at 3°C, heated loungers, relaxation areas, reading room, garden terrace, premium towels, robes, organic amenities, and complimentary juices and herbal tea. Come and go all day.

Do I need to book in advance?

No reservation needed! Walk-ins are our specialty—just show up whenever suits you and we'll take care of the rest. 99% of our guests simply walk in, explore the sanctuary, and stay as long as they like. Online booking is available if you prefer, but it's completely optional.

What should I bring?

Just yourself and swimwear. We provide everything else: premium towels, robes, slippers, organic skincare, hair care, and lockers for your belongings. Monthly members get a dedicated locker.

What are the benefits of cold plunge?

Cold exposure at 3°C boosts dopamine by 250% (lasting 2-3 hours), triggers anti-inflammatory responses, improves circulation, and enhances mental clarity. Research shows it reduces inflammation markers by 40% and accelerates recovery time significantly.

Is HBOT safe? Who is it for?

HBOT is safe for most healthy adults and is used by athletes and clinics worldwide. It's particularly beneficial for recovery, inflammation, and cognitive enhancement. Certain conditions require medical clearance—our staff will guide you.

Can I host a private event?

Absolutely. We host corporate wellness days, celebrations, and private groups from 6 to 50 guests. Full sanctuary buyouts include catering options and customized programming. Contact us to plan your event.
